About Me

I am an aspiring researcher in the fields of Psychology and Neuroscience. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD at the University of Genova (Italy). My research topics cover: math anxiety, math learning, and numerical cognition during the development. I am interested in executive functions and gender differences in various emotive and cognitive aspects.

Basically, I am keen on computer stuff in general, and I appreciate a little programming simple stuff. I spend my free time learning statistics.
